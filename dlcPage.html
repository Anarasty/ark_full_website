ARK: Scorched Earth - Expansion Pack

Stranded naked, dehydrated & starving in a vast desert, even the most seasoned ARK survivors must quickly find water, hunt for food, harvest, craft items, and build shelter to have any chance for survival. Use skills honed on ARK's faraway Island to kill, tame, breed, and ride the fantastical new creatures that have evolved to survive the Desert's ultra harsh conditions, including... DRAGONS! Travel back and forth between the Island and the Desert to team up with hundreds of players across both worlds or play locally!

ARK: Aberration - Expansion Pack

Waking up in Aberration - an abandoned, malfunctioning ARK with a complex underground biome system - the survivors will come face-to-face with new unparalleled challenges: radioactive sunlight and environmental hazards, ziplines, planning suits, cave dwellings, batteries and more, along with a host of incredible new creatures waiting in the mysterious depths. Beware of the Nameless: relentless, Element-enhanced humanoids transformed into vicious monsters! Survivors must discover what the future holds for those strong enough to survive!

ARK: Extinction - Expansion Pack

Complete your journey through the worlds of ARK in the "Extinction" add-on where the story begins and ends: on Earth itself! Contaminated by the Element, a ravaged planet filled with fantastic organic and technological creatures holds both the secrets of the past and the keys to salvation. Now the ultimate test awaits you, seasoned survivors who have overcome all previous obstacles: can you defeat the giant wandering titans that reign over the planet and complete the ARK cycle to save Earth's future?
